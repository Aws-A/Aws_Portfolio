My Journey

2017

Engineering

My background is in engineering. I have a B.Sc. in chemical engineering and obtained my certificate qualification from the Metropolitan University of Toronto in 2017. I’m also a member of PEO (Professional Engineers Ontario). I have worked in research and quality assurance.

This field has enhanced my research and analysis skills, enabling me to effectively tackle problem-solving tasks that I've applied to other aspects of my work.

Business

I have previously taken courses in business management. In 2019, I began to immerse myself further in the business field by enrolling in courses at the Metropolitan University of Toronto and pursuing an MBA program.

2020
