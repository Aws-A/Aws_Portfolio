Problem Statement

While users have numerous options in travel apps,
they often find themselves investing a considerable
amount of time in selecting from these options,
and aligning their accommodations, dining,
activities, and transportation with their budgets.

UX Hypothesis

An app offering travel packages with a range of
prices and the option to customize travel
experiences within the same package by
providing a selection ofsuggestions to
choose from.
