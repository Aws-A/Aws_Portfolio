ToolShare

ToolShare is an app to share tools and items within the
community, it gives options of low charges or even out
of charge depends on the people desires

User Research

We created two proto-personas, then worked on our interviews, then we worked on empathy map:

Empathy Map

Based on our interviews, we did affinity diagram, then our empathy map, that helps us in user insights and features prioritization

My role:
I participated with my team in user reasech from proto-persona and interviews to our affinity diagram and empathy map

User Insights: Jovial Julia wants to learn more about home improvement so she is able to fix things at home without calling an expensive professional
for every little project.
She is willing to put in the effort and learns what needs to
be done, but finds purchasing all the tools needed is
financially difficult.

Persona

We created two personas:
One for the person who offers the item or the service,
and the other one is for the person who wants this item or this service

My role:
I did the proto-persona that we developed to persona after our research

Competitor Analysis

That was my role to work on this part by doing SWOT analysis. I worked on four applications, all of them don't offer services,
only items without sharing options

MyRent, rent4me (Direct Competitors):
They offer many items but they don't exist in all markets, and especially in Canada, they are so limited in items and locations

Home Depot and Kijiji (Indirect Competitors):
Home Depot is just about renting some tools, while Kijiji is so limited in renting stuff. Both has no reviews for rents.

Definition & Synthesis

At this point, we created storyboard, user journey map and user flow

Storyboard

I created this storyboard by using Adobe Illustrator. Julia has a problem in her door and wants to get help. she finds this application and gets help from someone within her commuinty.

I want to clarify here how it could be confusing when a situation happens and how it will be comfortable to have option to find tools and services for free or reasonable prices.

User Journey Map

Julia gets up and down while searching for help in the internet:

She felt confused when the situtaion happens, then she felt down for finding solution with extra money. But she feels excited when she knows there are apps to help her. The excitement turns to disapointment because of overwhelming apps and not finding suitable solutions for her situation. Later, she gets ToolShare which helps her to find solution for free or resonable prices.
