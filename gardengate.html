Problem Statement

As a customer, you have the option to purchase
books, gifts, and stationery from stores. However,
there often aren’t enough opportunities to experience
something truly unique and enjoyable while spending
time either in-store or virtually.

UX Hypothesis

We are creating a new customer experience, beginning
with an app, website, then a possible physical store. In
this enhanced experience, people can enjoy reading,
chatting, and engaging in activities surrounded by
nature, music, and coffee.
